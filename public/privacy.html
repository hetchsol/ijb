Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account (username, email, display name), uploading content, and making purchases (phone number for mobile money transactions). We do not collect or store credit/debit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide and maintain the Platform, process transactions, communicate with you about your account, and improve our services.

3. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including password hashing, JWT authentication, HTTPS encryption, and rate limiting. However, no system is completely secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with payment processors to complete transactions and as required by law.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to maintain your login session and download history. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Creators may delete their accounts and content at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at info@ijbventures.com.
